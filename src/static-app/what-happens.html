What Happens to my data?

The below points provide an overview of how your data is collected and used when you agree to join a pilot with Nexus. For more information see our Privacy Notice.

  • When you register for a pilot, we will request your consent to process your personal data - you may withdraw this consent at any time.
  • On registration you will need to download our Nexus App and create an account with Nexus.
  • To set up the account we will collect information about who you are and how to contact you. We will also receive your NHS number from your NHS Trust.
  • When taking part in the pilot we will request information about your medical condition and ethnicity (this is deemed sensitive data and we only do this with your consent).
  • We will also conduct interviews with you about your experience of the pilot and App. Recordings and outcomes of these interviews are then stored securely.
  • We will share the medical information provided with your NHS Trust, as well as the results from the pilot.
  • After a pilot ends or you leave a pilot, we will not keep any personal data unless you choose to receive other services from us.
  • We may use aggregated and anonymous data obtained from the pilot for ethical research purposes.
  • The NHS Trust is the data controller and will retain your personal data after a pilot, under their usual policies and procedures.
